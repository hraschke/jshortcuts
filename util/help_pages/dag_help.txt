NAME
     dag -- Open diagnostic pages for a SmartEdge Gateway.

SYNOPSIS
     dag [ip address] [interface]

DESCRIPTION
     Use dag to quickly access the diagnostic pages for an Altair Engineering
     SmartEdge Gateway that is on your local LAN.

     Does not support options at this time (though this is slated for future
     changes). Configure the endpoint for diagnostic pages in
     $JSHOR/util/.jshor_config.
